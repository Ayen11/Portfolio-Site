About Me

I’m Emanuel. I started coding right before covid started at 2020. At first all I made were simple Twitter/Discord/Reddit bots in Python, but when covid hit I wanted to do something bigger.. So I started making games! I’ve worked through the 2 years of lockdowns on my first game IDIOTIC (The Game), and had some success with it. With a small community of 600 users on Reddit and a viral trailer that got to the top 10 posts on Reddit for that day.

Now I’m mastering new technologies like Unity and Godot and also web development by making web games and websites. I’ve already published one web game and made *this* website, and I’m looking forward to making more!
